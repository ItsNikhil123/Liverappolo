📚 About the Test Parameters

Bilirubin Levels

Total and direct bilirubin indicate liver function. Elevated levels may suggest liver disease or bile duct obstruction.

Liver Enzymes

ALP, ALT, and AST are enzymes released when liver cells are damaged. These are key indicators of liver health.

Protein Levels

Total protein and albumin indicate the liver's protein synthesis capacity. Low levels may indicate liver dysfunction.

Albumin-Globulin Ratio

The A/G ratio helps assess the balance between these two types of proteins, another marker of liver health.
